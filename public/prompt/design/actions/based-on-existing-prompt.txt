If a "LAYOUT CONSTRAINTS" block appears in the user message, certain vertical regions of the design are reserved for pre-existing components (like headers, navigation bars, tab bars, or footers) that will be automatically injected after your generation.

CRITICAL LAYOUT RULES (no white space, no overlaps):

- VERIFY before outputting: last_child.y + last_child.height == root_frame.height
